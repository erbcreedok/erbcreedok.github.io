    Kolsai-Kaindy

    Kolsai lakes, the so-called pearls of the Northern Tien Shan, represent a cascade of three mountain lakes. Here, you will be enjoying by the stunning and picturesque beauty of the pristine nature. Within the two days tour you will visit another no less beautiful than Kolsai Lake - Kaindy, a truly amazing example of the creation of nature. Anyone who has ever visited these beautiful places in Almaty, will never forget their splendor.
